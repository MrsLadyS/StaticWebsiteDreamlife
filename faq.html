Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Dreamlife Sim designed for?

Financial institutions such as credit unions, banks, and brokerages that run community financial education programs and want ongoing engagement for members ages 18–40.

What problem does this solve after workshops or classes end?

It keeps momentum going by turning education into ongoing, goal-based action so members continue progress between events instead of stalling.

Is Dreamlife Sim a course or an app?

It’s a self-guided digital app experience designed to be used before, during, and after education moments.

How does Dreamlife integrate with programs we already run?

Dreamlife is designed to extend your existing education efforts into a digital experience without adding staff or complexity. A demo will clarify the best integration approach for your program.

What does pricing look like?

Pricing is based on licensing plus a per-user cost. Request a demo to discuss partner packages and get exact pricing.

What do members actually do inside the experience?

Members visualize goals, map pathways, and take weekly micro-actions that keep progress moving over time.

How do we measure engagement or outcomes?

Dreamlife includes engagement insights so you can see member participation and identify what’s working to improve results over time.

What is the best next step if we’re interested?

Schedule a demo. You’ll see the experience, review partner options, and discuss how Dreamlife fits your existing education strategy.
