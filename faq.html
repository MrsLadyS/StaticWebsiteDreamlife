Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Dreamlife-Sim designed for?

Financial institutions such as credit unions, banks, and brokerages that run community financial education programs and want ongoing engagement for members ages 18–40.

What problem does this solve after workshops or classes end?

It keeps momentum going by turning education into ongoing, goal-based action so members continue progress between events instead of stalling. It also reaches adults who can't or don't have time for in-person events—Dreamlife-Sim brings your resources and connection to them when they need it, so you engage people who would otherwise stay out of reach.

Is Dreamlife-Sim a course or an app?

It’s a self-guided digital app experience designed to be used before, during, and after education moments.

How does Dreamlife-Sim integrate with programs we already run?

Dreamlife-Sim is designed to extend your existing education efforts into a digital experience without adding staff or complexity. A demo will clarify the best integration approach for your program.

What does pricing look like?

Pricing is based on licensing plus a per-user cost. Request a demo to discuss partner packages and get exact pricing.

What do members actually do inside the experience?

Members visualize goals, map pathways, and take weekly micro-actions that keep progress moving over time.

How do we measure engagement or outcomes?

Dreamlife-Sim provides aggregated engagement insights—participation trends, return visits, and signals of what members are working toward (e.g., homebuying, saving for college)—so you can report on impact beyond one-time post-workshop surveys and demonstrate program value without surveying every member.

We have a small team and limited capacity. How does Dreamlife-Sim fit?

Dreamlife-Sim is built to extend your existing events without adding staff, extra workshops, or dedicated logistics. The app runs alongside what you already do; members get ongoing engagement between events, and you get insights without running more programs.

What is the best next step if we’re interested?

Schedule a demo. You’ll see the experience, review partner options, and discuss how Dreamlife-Sim fits your existing education strategy.
