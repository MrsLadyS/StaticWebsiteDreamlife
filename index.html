For financial institution leaders advancing community engagement and financial education

Turn one-time financial education events into an ongoing digital member experience.

Dreamlife-Sim is a digital extension of institution-led financial education that helps members visualize the life they want, map realistic pathways, and take small steps over time. It keeps your financial institution connected between workshops and life milestones without adding staff or complexity.

Financial institutions invest heavily in community-based financial education—but often hit limits: volunteer exhaustion, no clear metrics or long-term impact data, and no way to scale without adding headcount or more events. Dreamlife-Sim extends the impact of the events you already run, without burning out your team or your volunteers.

Are you ready to...

  • Extend the impact of Reality Fairs, homebuyer classes, and workshops without adding more events or exhausting volunteers
  • Reach more members with a digital experience—no extra in-person logistics or staff capacity required
  • Engage adults who can't or don't have time for in-person events—Dreamlife-Sim brings your resources and connection to them when they need it
  • Get engagement and participation insights so you can report on impact and show program value
  • Move beyond one-time post-workshop surveys and see how members stay engaged and take action over time
  • Reinforce learning with micro-actions and weekly tasks that fit busy adult lives
  • Help members apply what they learn as they use your institution’s tools, products, and services
