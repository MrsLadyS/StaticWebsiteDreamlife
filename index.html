What We Provide

Free National Standard Financial Education Courses

Flexible Accessible Standards-Aligned

We believe financial literacy is a right, not a privilege. That's why we've made all National Standard Financial Education Courses completely free for everyone. Simply register on the platform to unlock high-quality educational content that meets national standards, ensuring users are equipped with essential financial skills and knowledge.

Moneyling Financial Education Curriculum

Comprehensive Story-based Customizable

The stand-alone financial education curriculum offers a comprehensive, story-based financial literacy program designed for flexible implementation across various educational and community settings.

Week of Financial Literacy Program

Immersive Multi-Subject Five Days

Our flagship program, the Week of Financial Literacy, is an immersive five-day program designed for the engagement of all teachers. Each educator teaches the subjects they know best - Math, ELA, Social Studies, Art and more- through the lens of financial choices, goals, and knowledge.

Moneyling Connection Ceremony

Celebrate Learning Foster Connections Inspire Futures

Interactive event designed to celebrate student learning, featuring student scholarship awards, a student-moderated discussion panel with industry experts, and partnership fair that connects students with local businesses, community leaders, financial institutions and career opportunities.

Moneyling Dreamlife Sim

AI-Powered Personalized Planning Actionable Steps

The Dreamlife Sim helps you visualize your future, plan your path, and live your dream. Using AI, it turns you goals into personalized images, local cost insights, and career pathways. With weekly tasks and decision-making tools, it transforms financial literacy into real-world action- making dreams achievable one step at a time.

Empowering Communities

The Moneyling LMS is not just for schools. Community groups and institutions can also access these transformative programs to foster financial literacy and bridge gaps in education. By connecting participants to local resources and opportunities, Moneyling empowers individuals to take control of their financial futures while building stronger, more informed communities.
