Dreamlife Sim for Credit Unions

Dreamlife Sim is a mobile-first web experience that extends your financial education program. Members explore their dream lifestyle, career path, and retirement plan through a guided simulation that converts curiosity into concrete next steps.

What Members Get Inside the App

Members move through three core experiences: Dreamlife Visualization, Weekly Micro-Lessons and Tasks, and Community Connection. The app includes lifestyle cost planning, career pathway exploration, retirement projection tools, SMART goals, and shareable progress moments that keep them engaged.
