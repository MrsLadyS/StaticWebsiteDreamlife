About Dreamlife Sim

Developed by Moneyling, Dreamlife Sim extends your institution's financial education programs with interactive, visual learning experiences that engage members and drive results.

Transform Abstract Goals Into Tangible Plans

Dreamlife Sim helps your institution members see what's possible by transforming abstract financial goals into tangible, visual simulations. Our platform seamlessly connects members with your existing educational resources, financial advisors, and peer community support.

As a B2B2C platform, Dreamlife integrates directly into your institution's member wellness programs, complementing your existing financial literacy initiatives with cutting-edge technology and gamified engagement.

  • Designed specifically for Credit Unions and Financial Institutions
  • Targets members ages 18-40 seeking financial wellness
  • Extends existing education programs, doesn't replace them
  • Seamless integration with institution resources and advisors
