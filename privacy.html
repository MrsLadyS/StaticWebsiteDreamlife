Introduction

Moneyling, Inc. ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Dreamlife Sim mobile application and web platform (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our educational financial planning platform.

Important: Dreamlife Sim is an educational tool designed to teach financial literacy. We do not provide financial advice, brokerage services, or investment recommendations.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, first name (for personalization), and password
  • Profile Information: Age, location, and educational preferences
  • Simulation Data: Your lifestyle, career, and retirement planning inputs (optional and should not include personal identifiers)
  • Communication Data: Messages you send to our support team

Data We Collect for Identity

  • First Name: For personalization purposes only
  • Email Address: For account management and communication
  • No Last Names: We do not collect last names
  • Optional Simulation Inputs: Additional simulation inputs are optional and should not include personal identifiers

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Analytics: App interactions, feature usage, and session duration
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Performance Data: App crashes, loading times, and error logs
  • Location Data: General location (city/state) for lifestyle cost calculations

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Financial Account Information: Bank accounts, credit cards, or investment accounts
  • Social Security Numbers: We never request or store SSNs
  • Detailed Personal Information: Addresses, phone numbers, or other sensitive identifiers

How We Use Your Information

Educational Purpose

  • Simulation Processing: Generate personalized financial planning scenarios
  • Learning Analytics: Track your progress and learning outcomes
  • Content Personalization: Recommend relevant educational materials
  • Platform Improvement: Enhance features based on usage patterns

Service Operation

  • Account Management: Maintain your user account and preferences
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and provide assistance
  • Security: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • Legal Compliance: Meet regulatory requirements and legal obligations

Analytics and Research

  • Aggregate Analysis: Study user behavior patterns (anonymized)
  • Product Development: Improve our educational tools and features
  • Educational Research: Contribute to financial literacy research (anonymized)
  • Aggregated/De-identified Use: We may use your inputs, reports, and AI-generated outputs in aggregated, de-identified form (including randomized or otherwise non-identifiable transformations) to operate, improve, and develop the Services and to generate statistics and insights

Age and Eligibility (18+)

Minimum Age Requirement

  • Minimum Age: You must be 18 years or older to create an account and use the app
  • Adult-Only Service: We do not create accounts for users under 18 in the consumer app
  • Age Verification: By creating an account, you confirm you are 18+ and agree to our Terms and Privacy

Limited Educational Use Exception (Schools Only)

For compliance coverage (CYA) - applies only when an educational institution is explicitly onboarded and provides access codes under a separate written agreement:

  • Institution-Managed Use: Where an institution issues access codes for classroom use, the school/instructor is responsible for ensuring students use anonymous, non-identifiable information in profiles and inputs
  • Data Minimization: Moneyling's systems for school use do not require student names, personal emails, or other direct identifiers; pseudonymous IDs are recommended
  • Compliance Allocation: The institution is responsible for obtaining any required parental consents and for COPPA/FERPA compliance. Moneyling acts as a service provider/processor to the institution under the separate agreement
  • No Advertising: No behavioral advertising to students; data collection limited to delivering the educational service

Parental Use and Youth Simulation Data

  • Adult Users (18+): May simulate scenarios about a child (e.g., first job to retirement)
  • Generic Descriptors: Simulations may display ages younger than 18 for modeling purposes
  • No Child Identifiers: Do not include a child's personal identifiers in any inputs. Use generic descriptors only (e.g., "Child A," age, contribution amount)
  • Long-Horizon Modeling: Moneyling permits long-horizon modeling (e.g., compounding to 80 years) for planning; this does not create a child account
